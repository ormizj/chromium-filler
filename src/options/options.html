      Two-step applications

      Postings that apply on the employer's own site are followed automatically, after any “before leaving” steps for that site (e.g. clicking its Save job button). Both the posting and the application it leads to are recorded.

      Review modal — size & position

      Drag the card to move it; drag its top-left corner to resize, or its left or top edge to resize one direction only. Edges snap to the screen edge and to the default gutter, and an edge that has run out of room lights up — indigo against the screen, amber at the smallest usable size. With the keyboard, focus the card and use the arrow keys (hold Shift for 10px); focus a handle to resize with them instead.

      The frame below is your screen to scale — the display minus the space your browser's own tab strip, address bar and bookmarks bar take, measured when this page loaded — so what you set here is what the modal gets on a real posting. Resizing this window does not change it. This applies on desktop only: under 640px wide the modal is always a full-width bottom sheet, so on a phone the frame stands in for a desktop screen rather than for this one.

      The preview renders in this window, so if it is smaller than the screen above, the card is clamped to fit it.
